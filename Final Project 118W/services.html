Services

Starting with excellent matchmaking services from our adoption counselors, an HSSV adoption includes: Spay/Neuter surgery All necessary vaccines appropriate for the animal’s age and routine deworming Home Again Microchip with life-time registration Sponsored veterinary visit and select aftercare discount through one of our partners Expert tips on pet care and training, including free behavior advice for the lifetime of the animal from a behavior specialist Post Adoption advice as your new pet adjusts to the home All our animals have been examined by veterinary staff and received a behavior assessment


Pet Sitting

Our pet sitting service allows your pet to remain in a familiar, secure environment – your home – while you travel! Our sitter will schedule an initial meeting to gain a clear understanding of your specific needs and to establish trust and confidence with you and your pet. Our visits last approximately thirty minutes each, during which time the sitter will follow your pet’s customary diet and exercise routine and provide personal attention and play time.


Volunteering Services

The animals need you! Volunteers provide daily animal care, dog walking, cat socialization, and adoption support. Opportunities include weekly, daily, and on-call shifts at the shelter.


Animals Looking for a new home


Our dog walking service is designed to help pet parents who work long hours or have after work commitments that can make for an extra long day for their pet or those that have young or elderly pets that would benefit from a bathroom break. Our thirty minute visit focuses on meeting the core needs of your pet while offering an opportunity for extra exercise, play time and attention while you are away.
